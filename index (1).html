Multither: Multiply and Play!

🎮 Game Rules

  1. Objective: Guide the snake to eat the correct answer to each multiplication question.
  2. Movement: Use the arrow keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to control the snake.
  3. Question Display: A multiplication question appears at the top. Choose the correct product from the answer options on the board.
  4. Scoring: +10 points for each correct answer; -1 point for each wrong answer or wall hit; bonus points awarded based on snake length at the end.
  5. Duration: The game ends after 60 seconds or when 20 questions are answered.
  6. End Result: Your final score includes accuracy, speed, and bonus points.
